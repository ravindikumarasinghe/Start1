Corona Virus

SYMPTOMS

Signs and symptoms of COVID-19 may appear two to 14 days after the exposure and can include:
  • Fever
  • Cough
  • Shortness of breath or difficulty in breathing
  • Aches
  • Runny nose
  • Sore Throat
  • Tiredness
  • Diarrhea
  • Vomiting
